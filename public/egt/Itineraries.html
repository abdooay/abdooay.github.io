Family Trip 1

Day 1:

  • Depart on an overnight flight to Cairo, Egypt.

Day 2:

  • Arrive at Cairo Airport and transfer to the hotel.

Day 3:

  • Morning drive to Memphis, visit the colossal statue of Ramses II.
  • Proceed to the Necropolis Sakkara and visit the Step Pyramid of King Zoser.
  • Lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Visit the Pharaonic Village.

Day 4:

  • Visit the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx.
  • Tour the Egyptian Museum of Antiquity, including King Tut's treasures.
  • Dinner with an Egyptian family.

Day 5:

  • Tour Medieval Cairo: Sultan Hassan Madrassa and Mosque, Coptic Cairo.
  • Afternoon rest at the hotel.

Day 6:

  • Flight to Aswan. Tour the High Dam and Philae Temple. Check into the hotel.

Day 7:

  • Short Felucca ride around Aswan islands. Bus journey to Luxor. Check into the hotel for three nights.

Day 8:

  • Tour Valley of the Kings, Valley of the Nobles, and Memnon Colossi.
  • Visit the Mummification Museum.

Day 9:

  • Visit Karnak Temple.
  • Free afternoon.
  • Evening visit to Luxor Temple.

Day 10:

  • Flight back to Cairo. Farewell dinner.

Day 11:

  • Flight home.

HMSC Egypt and Sudan

Egypt Segment:

Day 1:

  • Depart for Cairo, arrive the next evening.

Day 2:

  • Arrive in Cairo, transfer to Mena House hotel.

Day 3:

  • Early morning visit to the pyramids and Sphinx.
  • Tour the Solar Boat Museum.
  • Visit the Egyptian Museum of Antiquities.

Day 4:

  • Tour Memphis and Sakkara.
  • Visit the pyramids at Dashur.

Day 5:

  • Drive to Fayoum, visit Medinet Madi and Wadi al-Hitan.

Day 6:

  • Drive to Bahariya Oasis. Visit the Valley of the Golden Mummies.

Day 7:

  • Drive to Farafra Oasis. Tour the White Desert. Camp in the White Desert.

Day 8:

  • Drive to Dakhla Oasis. Visit Mut and other villages.

Day 9:

  • Drive to Kharga Oasis. Tour Kharga Oasis and Roman fortresses.

Days 10-12:

  • Drive to Luxor. Tour Karnak and Luxor Temples, Valley of the Kings, Valley of the Queens.

Day 13:

  • Optional hot-air balloon ride. Tour the Luxor Museum. Farewell dinner.

Day 14:

  • Fly to Cairo or Aswan. Continue to Sudan or return home.
